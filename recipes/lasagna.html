Lovely Lush Lasagna

A Classic in the Richard's home!

Fat Mince. Fat Cheese. Fat Pasta. Fat Martoes, Fat Bessymel. Lovely stuff!

ingrediants
  • 500g Mince
  • 500g Cheese
  • 500g Pasta Sheets
  • 500g Tin Tommies
  • 500g Salt
Steps
  1. Fry that mince.
  2. Chuck in the Tomatoes and the Salt.
  3. Boil some water and get those sheets soft.
  4. Chuck the beefy marto mixture in a dish.
  5. Put in a cheeky bit of Bessamel.
  6. Layer some pasta.
  7. Probably best to do all that twice so it's abit thicker.
  8. Whack it in the oven for like half hour?
