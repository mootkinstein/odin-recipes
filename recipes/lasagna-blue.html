Lovely Lush Blue Lasagna

A Blue Classic in the Richard's home!

Blue Mince. Blue Cheese. Blue Pasta. Blue Martoes, Blue Bessymel. Lovely stuff!

ingrediants
  • 500g Mince
  • 500g Cheese
  • 500g Pasta Sheets
  • 500g Tin Tommies
  • 500g Salt
  • Blue Dye
Steps
  1. Fry that mince.
  2. Chuck in the Tomatoes and the Salt.
  3. Add Half the Blue Dye
  4. Boil some water and blue dye and get those sheets soft.
  5. Chuck the beefy marto mixture in a dish.
  6. Put in a cheeky bit of Bessamel.
  7. Layer some pasta.
  8. Probably best to do all that twice so it's abit thicker.
  9. Whack it in the oven for like half hour?
